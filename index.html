About ShaBe

ShaBe started from a love of colour, texture and small joyful things. From beaded bracelets to cozy candles and illustrated prints, each piece is made by hand with attention to detail and a bit of playful colour. Catch ShaBe at local markets — or send a message to request a custom order.

Crafts include: Candles, Beaded Jewellery, Printed Shirts, Tote Bags, Illustrations, Handmade Soap and more.
